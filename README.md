# Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Objective
This project focuses on ecommerce sales analysis, customer behaviour analysis, promotional analysis, and product performance analysis using data cleaning, dashboarding, and business intelligence techniques.

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Dashboarding
- Business Intelligence

## Project Workflow
1. Raw Data Collection
2. Data Cleaning & Transformation
3. Audit & Validation
4. Dashboard Creation
5. Business Insight Generation
6. Recommendations & Reporting

## Key Insights
- Organic products generated higher revenue compared to sponsored products.
- Battery and utility products contributed the highest revenue.
- Excellent-rated products generated major revenue contribution.
- Coupon-based purchases contributed relatively low revenue.
- Electronics and tech accessories showed strong customer demand.

## Files Included
- raw_data.csv
- audit_table.csv
- cleaned_data.csv
- dashboard_image.png
- Amazon_Ecommerce_Analytics_Report.pdf

## Limitations
- Missing shipping date values affected delivery analysis.
- Revenue distribution was positively skewed.
- Limited coupon transaction data restricted promotional analysis depth.

## Recommendations
- Improve sponsored product targeting strategy.
- Expand high-performing product categories.
- Improve shipment tracking and operational records.
- Enhance customer segmentation and promotional analysis.
